[h1]SimpleCompass[/h1]

Never lose your bearings in Kenshi again. SimpleCompass adds a compact, clickable compass HUD element to the game screen showing your current heading in degrees and cardinal direction.

[b]Requirements[/b]
[list]
[*][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/kenshi/mods/847]RE_Kenshi[/url] (tested on v0.34)
[*]Kenshi v1.0.65+
[/list]

[b]How to Use[/b]
[list=1]
[*]Load any save or start a new game
[*]Look at the top-center of the screen - the compass is always visible
[*]Click the compass button to cycle between display modes
[/list]

[b]Display Modes[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Full[/b]: A rotating compass strip with tick marks for every 15 degrees, cardinal direction labels (N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW), a red caret indicator pointing at your exact bearing, and the numeric bearing with direction name below
[*][b]Number with Direction[/b]: Compact display showing just the bearing in degrees and the direction name (e.g. "45(NE)")
[*][b]Direction Only[/b]: Minimal display showing only the cardinal or intercardinal direction (e.g. "N", "SE")
[/list]

[b]Features[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Non-intrusive[/b] - Designed to be simple, small and placed near the top of the screen, out of the way
[*][b]Use the display mode you like best[/b] - Click the compass to switch between Full, Number with Direction, and Direction Only modes

[/list]

[b]Compatibility[/b]
[list]
[*]No special load order required beyond RE_Kenshi
[*]Does not alter any game files or UI elements beyond the added compass widget
[/list]

[b]Troubleshooting[/b]
[list]
[*]Confirm RE_Kenshi is installed and enabled
[*]If the compass does not appear, verify the mod is activated in the launcher
[/list]
